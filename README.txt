In my Frat simulator game, there are many things to keep track of

Reputation: this is determined by the amount of good repsonses you deliver the frat guys in your conversations

Health: this is your level of drunkenness. You will experience more drunkenness as you walk around the frat and talk to people


Shop: You can open this  in the same spot as the chats and info, this can help you get a ID which unlocks secret dialog

Dialog: These are conversations you have with the frat brothers(stroggs) which will determine your reputation.
